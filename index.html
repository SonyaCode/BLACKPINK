BLACKPINK (Hangul: 블랙핑크) is a South Korean girl group under YG Entertainment, consisting of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They debuted on August 8th, 2016 with their first single album, Square One, which contained “Whistle” and “Boombayah”.

Group Position Explanation

Main Vocalist

The Main Vocalist is the member with the best singing skill, who gets the most difficult vocal parts.

Lead Vocalist

The Lead Vocalist is the member with the 2nd best singing skill. They usually sing before the Main Vocalist and support the Main Vocalist.

Sub-Vocalist

The Sub-Vocalist supports the Main and Lead Vocalists and can get fewer singing lines.

Main Rapper

The Main Rapper is the member with the best rapping skills, and gets most of the rapping parts.

Lead Rapper

The Lead Rapper is the member with the 2nd best rapping skill. They usually begin the rapping parts.

Main Dancer

The Main Dancer is the member with the greatest dancing skills. The Main Dancer usually gets solo dancing parts and center parts during dance breaks.

Lead Dancer

The Lead Dancer is the 2nd best dancer in the group. When the group dances together, they will often dance in the front.

Visual

The Visual is the member who fits the Korean beauty standards the most.
